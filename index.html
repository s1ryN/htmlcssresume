About me ———

Alice is a sensible prepubescent girl from a wealthy English family who finds herself in a strange world ruled by imagination and fantasy. Alice feels comfortable with her identity and has a strong sense that her environment is comprised of clear, logical, and consistent rules and creatures. Alice's familiarity with the world has led one critic to describe her as a "disembodied intellect." Alice displays great curiosity and attempts to fit her diverse experiences into a clear understanding of the world.
